Your Journey

Skills & Roadmap

A clear, structured path from beginner to AI or ICT professional — built for tech students. Follow the stages below and build your career step by step during your degree.

Step by Step

Your Learning Roadmap

STAGE 01

Foundations

Learn Python, basic statistics, and linear algebra. These are the bedrock of every AI career. Complete a Python bootcamp and work through Khan Academy's statistics course.

STAGE 02

Data & SQL

Master data manipulation with Pandas and NumPy. Learn SQL for querying databases. Practise on real datasets from Kaggle. Build your first data analysis project.

STAGE 03

Machine Learning

Study supervised and unsupervised learning algorithms. Use Scikit-learn and work through Andrew Ng's Machine Learning course. Complete at least three ML projects.

STAGE 04

Deep Learning

Dive into neural networks with TensorFlow or PyTorch. Study CNNs, RNNs, and Transformers. Follow the fast.ai course for practical deep learning experience.

STAGE 05

Specialise

Choose your niche — NLP, Computer Vision, MLOps, or Cybersecurity. Build a portfolio project in your chosen area that solves a real problem.

STAGE 06

Certify & Apply

Earn at least one industry-recognised certification (IBM, Google, or AWS). Polish your GitHub, update your CV, and start applying for AI roles or internships in the GCC.

Coding Your Path to Success

⏱️ Estimated Timeline

With consistent effort of 10–15 hours per week:

Stages 1 – 2 (Foundations) 2 – 3 months
Stage 3 (Machine Learning) 2 – 3 months
Stages 4 – 5 (DL + Specialise) 3 – 4 months
Stage 6 (Certify + Apply) 1 – 2 months

Get Certified

Recommended Certifications

Industry-recognised credentials that employers in the GCC actively look for in students and graduates.

IBM

IBM AI Engineering Professional Certificate

Covers ML, deep learning, and deployment on IBM Watson. Highly respected in the GCC job market.

12 months

Google

Google Professional ML Engineer


Validates your ability to design and build ML models on Google Cloud Platform. Globally recognised.

3–6 months prep

AWS

AWS Certified Machine Learning Specialty

Demonstrates expertise in building, training, and deploying ML models on AWS infrastructure. Perfect for AI professionals.

3–6 months prep

DeepLearning.AI

Deep Learning Specialisation

Andrew Ng's flagship course — the gold standard introduction to neural networks and deep learning. Kickstart your deep learning journey.

4 months

Microsoft

Azure AI Fundamentals

A great entry-level certification covering core AI concepts and Azure AI services. Ideal for beginners.

1–2 months

CompTIA

CompTIA Security+

The most recognised entry-level cybersecurity certification, essential for Cyber Analyst and IT Support roles.

2–3 months
