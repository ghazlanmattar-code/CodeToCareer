AI & ICT Careers Across the GCC

The Gulf Cooperation Council is investing billions into AI, smart cities, and digital transformation. Discover where the opportunities are for tech students!

Country Spotlight

Where to Build Your Career?

Each GCC country has a unique ICT and AI strategy and job market. Here is what tech students need to know:

United Arab Emirates

Hub: Dubai & Abu Dhabi

The UAE has a dedicated Ministry of Artificial Intelligence and aims to be a global AI hub by 2031. Home to Mohamed bin Zayed University of AI — the world's first graduate university dedicated to AI.

Avg. Salary AED 25,000 – 55,000/mo

Saudi Arabia

Hub: Riyadh & NEOM

Vision 2030 is driving one of the largest digital transformation programmes in history. NEOM — the $500B smart city project — is creating thousands of AI and tech roles for graduates.

Avg. Salary SAR 18,000 – 45,000/mo

Qatar

Hub: Doha & Qatar Science & Technology Park

Qatar National Vision 2030 is building a knowledge-based economy. QCRI is a world-class AI research hub offering student internships. The Qatar Foundation supports tech talent through scholarships.

Avg. Salary QAR 18,000 – 40,000/mo

Bahrain

Hub: Manama FinTech Bay

Bahrain is the GCC's leading FinTech and cloud hub. Tamkeen offers generous funding for Bahraini nationals pursuing tech careers and certifications — up to 100% of training costs covered.

Avg. Salary BHD 1,200 – 3,500/mo

Kuwait

Hub: Kuwait City

Kuwait Vision 2035 is prioritising tech and AI to diversify from oil. The Kuwait Foundation for the Advancement of Sciences funds research and innovation programmes for students and graduates.

Avg. Salary KWD 900 – 2,800/mo

Oman

Hub: Muscat

Oman Vision 2040 positions AI as a key pillar of economic diversification. ITA Oman is the national digital transformation authority investing in smart infrastructure and youth talent programmes.

Avg. Salary OMR 900 – 2,500/mo

Funding Your Future

Scholarships & Student Programmes

You do not have to fund your education alone. These programmes support GCC students and graduates.

Tamkeen Training Support

Bahrain — Up to 100% funded

Tamkeen subsidises training and certification costs for Bahraini nationals, including AI and cloud certifications from IBM, AWS, and Microsoft.

Qatar Foundation Scholarships

Qatar — Fully Funded

Qatar Foundation offers grants for students pursuing STEM and AI-related degrees at Education City universities including Carnegie Mellon and Georgetown.

MBZUAI Graduate Fellowships

UAE — Fully Funded + Stipend

Mohamed bin Zayed University of AI offers fully funded graduate fellowships in ML, Computer Vision, and NLP for exceptional students worldwide.
